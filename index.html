INDIAN SOCIETY FOR TECHNICAL EDUCATION

ABOUT US

The Indian Society for Technical Education – NITK Chapter is one of the oldest technical clubs at NITK that is dedicated to providing enthusiastic students with an avenue to explore interests in fields apart from their academics. With various Special Interest Groups (SIG’s) constituted in the club, the members have the opportunity to truly branch out from their academics and explore other options that may contribute to their careers, or just out of their love for learning. The SIG’s housed within ISTE – NITK are Charge (Electronics), Crypt (Computer Science and IT), Chronicle (Literature), Credit (Commerce), Concrete (Civil), Clutch (Mechanical) and Create (Graphic Design), with recruitments conducted every year for the same. Besides this, the club also conducts events, workshops and seminars throughout the year for students within NITK.

  • CHARGE

    Microcontrollers, Robotics, IoT, LEDs! If these words resonate with you, and you just can't resist your fascination for electronics, ISTE's Charge SIG is the place to be. With innovative seminars, interesting projects and hands-on workshops, this SIG has soldered itself to the hearts of many. We aim to embed an enthusiasm towards electronics in NITK. We also take pride in actively contributing to ISTE fest Transcend and the mega event Technites during ENGINEER.







  • CREDIT

    Credit at ISTE – NITK is the commerce SIG that helps students get in touch with topics such as entrepreneurship, marketing, finance and economics. The SIG conducts knowledge exchange programs and undertakes several projects that cover various branches in commerce giving its members an opportunity to experience another perspective. Credit has also conducted events on campus in collaboration with other SIG’s, such as Spectacle and PowerPlay.







  • CLUTCH

    If your vision makes you look at the planes and Quadcopters in the sky, ships on the water and cars on the road and desire to unfold the knowledge hidden behind these machines, we the Clutch SIG of ISTE are the ones you're looking for! This SIG focuses on design and dynamics of machines, and conducts workshops and knowledge sharing sessions for the better understanding of them.
